Serves: 2 people

Recommended Serving Size: 1/2

Skill Level: 3/5

Nutrional Value (1/2)
Calories Fat (grams) Carbohydrates (grams) Protein (grams)
767 36.9 73.2 29.6

Catering to dietary needs:

  • Celiacs (Gluten Free): use gluten free corn chips instead
  • Vegetarians: Substitute chicken with kidney beans

Ingredients

  • 250 grams chicken mince
  • 1 small onion â€“ diced finely
  • 1/4 c tomato paste
  • 1/2 c tomato salsa
  • 1/4 tsp chilli powder
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • 1 tsp coriander
  • 1/2 bag of corn chips
  • 1/2 c Cheese
  • Guacamole (Optional)
  • Sour cream (Optional)

Method

  1. Heat frying pan, cook meat, breaking up lumps with spoon (Tip: Keep stirring so the chicken does not stick to the pan)

  2. Stir in onion, chilli, cumin & coriander. Cook over medium heat for 1 minute

  3. Add tomato paste & salsa, simmer 2 mins

  4. Arrange corn chips around serving plate, spoon meat mixture into centre. Sprinkle with cheese.

  5. Place under grill until cheese melts or bake in over 200°C

  6. Serve with guacamole and sour cream if you wish

  7. Voila!
