Serves: 4 people

Recommended Serving Size: 400g

Skill Level: 2/5

Nutrional Value (400g)
Calories Fat (grams) Carbohydrates (grams) Protein (grams)
139 5.1 20.3 3.2

Catering to dietary needs:

  • Celiacs (Gluten Free): Bread can be replaced by gluten free bread
  • Vegetarian

Ingredients

  • 2 cup mixed ripe tomatoes
  • 1 small bunch fresh basil
  • Sea salt
  • Ground black pepper
  • Olive oil
  • White wine or herb vinegar
  • Grated parmesan

Method

  1. Preheat oven to 190°C

  2. Slice the bread 1cm thick and toast in the oven for 10-15 minutes (Tip: Remember to use oven mitts!)

  3. Lightly rub a cut clove of garlic, then drizzle with extra virgin olive oil and sprinkled with salt and pepper. Place aside.

  4. Wash the tomatoes, remove their cores and carefully squeeze out the seeds.

  5. Place in a bowl, tear the basil, season with salt and pepper, then add olive oil and vinegar to balance the flavours to your taste.

  6. Use a spoon to put the tomato mixture onto the toasted bread

  7. Top with parmesan cheese

  8. Voila!
