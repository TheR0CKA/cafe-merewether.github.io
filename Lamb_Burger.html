Serves: 4 people

Recommended Serving Size: 1 burger

Skill Level: 3/5

Nutrional Value (1 burger)
Calories Fat (grams) Carbohydrates (grams) Protein (grams)
242 16 7 18

Catering to dietary needs:

  • Celiacs (Gluten Free): Normal buns can be replaced by gluten free buns
  • High Cholesterol: Cook in sunflower or olive oil
  • Vegetarians: Substitute lamb with tofu, paneer or goat's cheese

Ingredients

    Lamb Pattie

  • 500 grams minced lamb
  • 1 stock cube
  • 1 small onion - diced
  • 1/4 cup breadcrumbs
  • 1 egg

    • Salad Mix

  • 4 slices cheese
  • 1 tomato (sliced)
  • 4 slices beetroot
  • 4 hamburger buns
  • BBQ or Tomato sauce

Method

  1. Combine beef, egg, stock cube, onion, breadcrumbs and egg, mix well.

  2. Divide mixture into 4, roll into balls and flatten slightly.

  3. Heat fry pan. Cook patties for 4 minutes on each side.(Tip: Use a spatula to flip the patties)

  4. Slice buns and grill to toast.

  5. Spread with BBQ or Tomato sauce; add cooked patties, cheese, tomato, beetroot and lettuce.

  6. Serve and enjoy.
