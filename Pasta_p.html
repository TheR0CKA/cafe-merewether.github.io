Serves: 4 people

Recommended Serving Size: 500g

Skill Level: 4/5

Nutrional Value (400g)
Calories Fat (grams) Carbohydrates (grams) Protein (grams)
604 14 92 23.4

Catering to dietary needs:

  • Celiacs (Gluten Free): Pasta can be replaced by gluten free pasta
  • Vegetarian

Ingredients

  • 2 red peppers, deseeded and sliced
  • 2 yellow peppers, deseeded and sliced
  • Extra virgin olive oil
  • Sea salt or table salt
  • Ground black pepper
  • 2 red onions, peeled and finely sliced
  • 2 cloves garlic, peeled and grated
  • Fresh parsley leaves finely chopped
  • 2 tablespoons red wine or balsamic vinegar
  • Grated parmesan
  • 2 heaped tablespoons mascarpone or sour cream (Optional)
  • 455g rigatoni, penne or spaghetti

Method

  1. Put a large frying pan on a medium heat with olive oil and a pinch of salt and pepper

  2. Cook all of the bell peppers in the pan for 15 minutes or until softened with the lid on

  3. Add the onion and cook for a further 20 minutes

  4. Add the garlic and parsley stalks, keeping everything moving in the pan. Cook for about 3 minutes most

  5. Taste and season with more salt and pepper

  6. Add the vinegar - it will sizzle away, so mix it with all of the vegetables

  7. Add one handful of the grated parmesan and the mascarpone or sour cream if you are using it and turn the heat down to minimum while you cook the pasta.

  8. Meanwhile put a large pot of salted water on to boil.

  9. Add the pasta to the boiling water and cook for 10-15 or until al dente (Tip: Keep checking after 10 minutes to see if it is cooked)

  10. When cooked, drain in a colander

  11. Put the peppers, pasta and parsley leaves into a large warmed bowl with some extra olive oil and mix

  12. Serve as is or with parmesan

  13. Enjoy!
