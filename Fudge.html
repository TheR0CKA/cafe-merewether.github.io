Serves: 20 portions

Recommended Serving Size: 4 portions

Skill Level: 1/5

Nutrional Value
Calories Fat (grams) Carbohydrates (grams) Protein (grams)
90 2.29 16.83 0.53

Catering to dietary needs:

  • High Cholesterol: Use margarine instead of butter
  • Diabetics: Use a sugar replacement

Ingredients

  • Melted butter to grease
  • 1 400g can sweetened condensed milk
  • 100g butter, cubed
  • 250g (1 1/4 cups, firmly packed) brown sugar
  • 1 tablespoon golden syrup
  • 100g good-quality dark chocolate, finely chopped

Method

  1. Brush a 20cm square pan with the melted butter to grease, then line with non-stick baking paper.

  2. Place the condensed milk, butter, brown sugar and golden syrup in a 3 litre (12-cup) heat-resistant glass bowl.

  3. Cook, uncovered for 10-12 minutes, stirring every 2 minutes, on Medium-High/ 650watts/70%, or until bubbles appear over the whole surface of the mixture.

  4. Add the chocolate and stir until smooth and well combined. Pour immediately into the prepared pan.

  5. Place in the fridge for 1 hour or until firm. Cut the fudge into 4cm x l0cm bars and then mark each into 2 portions using a sharp knife. Wrap in greaseproof paper and tie ends with string.

  6. Bon Appetite!
