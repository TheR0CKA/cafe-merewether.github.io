Serves: 4 people

Recommended Serving Size: 2 slices

Skill Level: 2/5

Catering to dietary needs:

  • Diabetics: Substitute sugar and compote with salt and pepper to taste
  • Celiacs (Gluten Free): Normal bread can be replaced by gluten free bread
  • High Cholesterol: Cook in sunflower or olive oil instead of butter

Ingredients

  • 125 grams strawberries
  • 120 grams blueberries
  • 1 T icing sugar
  • 6 eggs
  • 1 cup milk
  • 2 T caster sugar
  • 1t vanilla extract
  • 8 x 2cm thick slices of bread
  • 40 g butter

Method

  1. Wash and cut up strawberries into quarters, remembering to cut the tops off. Cover them with icing sugar and juice. Set aside.

  2. Crack eggs into bowl, then add milk, sugar and vanilla. Using a fork, whisk together until combined (Tip: Whisk in a fast circular motion). Pour into shallow dish that's large enough to hold bread in a single layer. Lay bread in the egg mixture and leave to soak for 10 minutes, turning once after 5 minutes, until the bread has absorbed the egg.

  3. Melt half the butter in a large non-stick frying pan over low- medium heat. Cook bread, in 2 batches for 2 minutes each side or until golden and egg has set. Divide toast among plates, and then serve with strawberries and blueberries.
