Serves: 4 people

Recommended Serving Size: 200g

Skill Level: 3/5

Nutrional Value
Calories Fat (grams) Carbohydrates (grams) Protein (grams)
583 35 48 18

Catering to dietary needs:

  • High Cholesterol: Use olive oil or sunflower oil instead of normal spray oil

Ingredients

  • 2 sheets frozen ready-rolled shortcrust pastry, partially thawed
  • 500g butternut pumpkin, peeled, cubed
  • 1 small red capsicum, thickly sliced
  • 1 small red onion, cut into thin wedges
  • Cooking spray
  • 4 eggs
  • 1/4 cup pure cream
  • 1/4 cup finely grated parmesan
  • 1 tablespoon chopped coriander

Method

  1. Preheat oven to 200°C/180°C fan-forced (Tip: Careful with the oven, use appropriate measures such as oven mitts). Place baking tray on top shelf of oven. Line another baking tray with baking paper.

  2. Use pastry to line base and sides of a 2.5cm-deep, 11.5cm x 34cm (base), loose-based fluted tart pan. Prick base with a fork. Freeze for 15 minutes or until firm.

  3. Place pumpkin, capsicum and onion on prepared tray. Spray with oil. Season with salt and pepper. Place on lower shelf of oven.

  4. Place tart pan on hot baking tray in oven. Bake for 10 to 15 minutes or until pastry is golden and vegetables are just tender.

  5. Remove tart pan and vegetables from oven. Reduce oven temperature to 180°C/160°C fan-forced.

  6. Place eggs, cream and cheese in a large jug. Whisk to combine. Season with salt and pepper.

  7. Arrange vegetables in pastry case. Pour over egg mix. Bake for 35 to 40 minutes or until golden and just set. (Tip: Allow 10-15 minutes of cooling time before serving) Garnish with coriander.

  8. Bon Appetite!
