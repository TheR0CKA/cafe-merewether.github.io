Serves: 2 people

Recommended Serving Size: 400g

Skill Level: 3/5

Nutrional Value (400g)
Calories Fat (grams) Carbohydrates (grams) Protein (grams)
472 28.8 12 41.6

Catering to dietary needs:

  • Celiacs (Gluten Free): Normal wraps and bread can be replaced by gluten free bread and wraps
  • High Cholesterol: Cook in sunflower or olive oil instead of butter
  • Vegetarians: Substitute chicken and bacon with tofu, paneer or goat's cheese

Ingredients

  • 2 slices white bread
  • 2 rashers bacon
  • 4 cos lettuce leaves
  • 1/2 cup shaved parmesan cheese
  • 1 small chicken breast (sliced)
  • 2 T Caesar salad dressing
  • 2 wholemeal wraps

Method

  1. Preheat oven to 190°C

  2. Remove the crust from the bread and dice into small cubes. Line oven tray with baking paper. Place bread cubes on oven tray and bake for 10 minutes

  3. Fry the bacon until crisp. Remove from pan and drain on absorbent paper (Tip: Use a spatula to flip the bacon)

  4. Cut the chicken breasts into coin-sized pieces. Tip: Make sure the pices are of similar sizes so they all cook at the same time

  5. Pan fry chicken breast until cooked. Remove from pan

  6. Tear the lettuce leaves and place in salad bowl. Mix with salad dressing

  7. Add bread cubes, bacon and chicken to the lettuce mixture

  8. Break the wraps into pieces and mix with the salad. Plate and serve

  9. Place half of the salad into one plate and half onto the other

  10. Top with parmesan cheese
